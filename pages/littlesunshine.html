Little Sunshine

Wordpress • CSS • UI • UX

About

Little Sunshine is a family home child care. This client was in need of a website for advertising its business in order to attract customers. Client required something clean and modern to stand out from the crowd. Wordpress was used for this website built locally through the use of wamp and a child theme.

Challenge

A lot of the things I used to build this website were a new concept to me and one which I had to learn as I worked. On top of that I had a deadline and was working alone.

Solution

Trying to figure out new things with a deadline and on my own was difficult. I needed to figure out how to work effeciently and manage to deliver a product that spoke well. To solve my dilemma, I investigated the quickest way to set up a child theme and start working on the website. Also finding certain plugins to help speed up the development process was monumental in my success.
