About

My personal web developement portfolio. This current portfolio is a redesign from an old design that I had up online. Built using the skeleton framework to help with development speed.

Challenge

Coming up with a design that I liked was tough. As a web developer I am never going to actually be done with my projects. I always find something new to improve or a new feature I would like to add.

Solution

Studying other developers portfolios was a great help when it came to building mine. Seeing what they included in their own portfolios gave me an idea of what things I needed and which things could be left out. After a few days of studying and taking notes on others designs I took everything that I liked and included it in my website.
